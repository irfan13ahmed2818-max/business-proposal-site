A great website won't magically grow a business, but it can make a real difference. When visitors trust your business within seconds, they're far more likely to contact you instead of leaving for a competitor.

If your current website is already bringing consistent results, there's no reason to change it. But if visitors are leaving without contacting you, your website may be costing you opportunities without you realizing it.
